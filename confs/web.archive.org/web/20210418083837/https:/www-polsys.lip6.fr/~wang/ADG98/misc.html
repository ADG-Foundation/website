Second International Workshop
on

Automated Deduction in Geometry

Beijing, China, August 1-3, 1998

Workshop location

The meeting will take place at Chai Tai International Center of Beijing University, located in the scientific village of the capital, where academic institutes, universities, and high-tech firms are gathered.

Arrival

Most major airlines have regular commercial flights to Beijing. Participants are encouraged to book their tickets as early as possible, because the ticket prices vary quite a lot depending on the time of reservation. We may pick you up at the airport if you could inform us of your arrival time and flight number in advance. Otherwise, you may take a taxi to the hotel, which costs about 120 Chinese Yuan (15 US dollars). The addresses of the hotels are given below.

Excursion and other social program

Beijing offers numerous places of cultural and historical interest for visitors. Not far from the conference site are the Summer Palace and the Winter Palace. In downtown Beijing, there are the Forbidden City and the Temple of Heaven. A tour to the Great Wall in the suburb of Beijing will be organized by the workshop free of charge for registered participants. Interested participants may attend the ASCM conference (August 6-8) and visit Dunhuang Mogao Grottoes located in the ancient silk road, and now the middle of a desert in the northwestern China. For the details, please consult http://www.lzu.edu.cn/landa/dept/comp/ascm98.html.
