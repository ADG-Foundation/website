Room reservation is automatically handled with registration and the price for accommodation during the conference (from Sep. 21 to Sep. 25) is included in the conference fee by default.

Note that if you are going to make your own arrangements on accommodation, your conference fee will be reduced accordingly. Please also let us know if you are in this case by email to zbzeng@sei.ecnu.edu.cn and mwu@sei.ecnu.edu.cn.
